The Oakland Patriots lacrosse team. Our game against Green Hill got called mid-match when a storm rolled in fast — the kind that leaves debris everywhere across Middle Tennessee.

Hey everyone, it's Lawson with Trash Titans.

This past week my Oakland Patriots lacrosse team played Green Hill out of Mt. Juliet. It was a great back-and-forth game, and we were all tied up heading into the third quarter when a massive storm came out of nowhere. Dark clouds rolled in, lightning started flashing, and the wind picked up so strong it started blowing our equipment across the sidelines. The game came to a screeching halt. We all ran for shelter in the locker rooms, and eventually the game had to be called. We still have to finish it later this season.

On the drive home, the damage was obvious everywhere. Downed trees, broken limbs scattered across yards, and debris blown into the roads. It was a clear reminder — storm season is here in Middle Tennessee.

You're Not Alone

If you've had trees or large limbs come down recently, you're not alone. Many of our customers have been reaching out with the same problem — big piles of yard debris they need gone fast. Here in the cities, you might have bulk debris pickup available, but it often sits on the roadside for a month or longer before the city gets around to it.

If you're hosting a graduation party, a family gathering, or you're simply tired of looking at the mess in your yard — waiting that long isn't realistic.

⚡ That's where we come in. At Trash Titans, we specialize in quick, reliable yard debris and storm cleanup. Same-week service, honest pricing, no runaround.

What We Offer After a Storm

We don't trim or cut trees ourselves, but once you (or your tree service) have cut the limbs and branches so they fit in our trailer, we handle everything from there. Here are the two main options:

Option 1 — Full Service Haul Away

Our team shows up, loads all the cut debris, and takes it off your property in one trip. Your yard is left clean and ready for whatever you have planned. This is the fastest option — often same-day or next-day.

Option 2 — Trailer Drop-Off

We drop a trailer at your house for a daily rate. You get a set number of days to load it on your own schedule. Place a deposit, load when it works for you, and we'll come pick it up when you're ready. This works especially well if you're in a county with no bulk pickup service at all.
