Junk Removal in Lebanon — Serving Wilson County

Lebanon is Wilson County's hub and one of the more underserved markets in Middle Tennessee when it comes to quality junk removal. A lot of the big franchise haulers concentrate on Nashville and the I-24 corridor — we cover Lebanon and Wilson County with the same fast response and honest pricing we give our Rutherford County customers.

Lebanon has a mix of old and new: historic homes near the town square and along Castle Heights, fast-growing subdivisions out toward Highway 109 and Coles Ferry Pike, and a solid industrial and commercial base along the corridors heading toward Nashville. We serve all of it — home cleanouts, garage overflows, commercial space turnovers, and rental property vacates.

We work regularly in areas like Hunters Point, Hartmann, and the neighborhoods around James E. Ward Agricultural Center. Lebanon customers often have older properties with decades of accumulated stuff in barns, outbuildings, and detached garages — we're comfortable with those kinds of jobs and we price them fairly based on volume, not the number of trips we guess we'll need.

Lebanon is about 30 minutes from Murfreesboro, and we route it alongside Mt. Juliet when the schedule allows. No distance surcharges, same pricing structure as everywhere else we serve. Text 615-987-9876 for a free quote — we're usually out to Lebanon within a week, often sooner.
