Our Story

Built By Hustle.
Driven By Results.

Lawson Gregory is a junior at Oakland High School in Murfreesboro, Tennessee — and he's already running a business that most adults would be proud of. Trash Titans didn't start with investors or a business plan. It started with a kid who saw a need in his community, grabbed a flatbed trailer, and got to work.

Every dollar Lawson earned went straight back into the business. That flatbed became a full-size dump trailer. Those first few neighbors became hundreds of satisfied customers across eight cities — from Murfreesboro driveways to Nashville rooftops and everywhere in between. He's hauled furniture, appliances, renovation debris, storm damage, office equipment, and some jobs that most haulers would turn down. No job is too unusual for Trash Titans.

"Jobs like this remind me how far hustle and hard work can take you." — Lawson Gregory

Trash Titans is fully insured and serves both residential and commercial customers across Middle Tennessee. When you hire Lawson, you're not calling a faceless corporation. You're supporting a local young entrepreneur who will show up on time, work hard, and make sure you're 100% satisfied — every single job.

The reviews speak for themselves. Neighbors tag him on Facebook. Customers send their kids out to help load the trailer. People call him a beast. He's just a kid from Murfreesboro who decided to build something — and he's doing exactly that.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about junk removal in Murfreesboro and Middle Tennessee.

How much does junk removal cost in Murfreesboro TN?

Pricing depends on the volume and type of items being removed. Trash Titans offers free, upfront quotes with no hidden fees. Fill out our quote form or text 615-987-9876 and we'll give you an honest price before we start.

What items does Trash Titans remove?

We haul almost anything — old furniture, appliances, garage junk, renovation debris, yard waste, storm damage, office equipment, and more. If it needs to go, we can haul it. No job is too big, too small, or too unusual.

How quickly can you pick up my junk in Murfreesboro?

We offer same-week service in most cases across Murfreesboro, Smyrna, LaVergne, Nashville, Nolensville, Franklin, Lebanon and Mt. Juliet. Fill out our quote form or text us and we'll get you scheduled fast.

Is Trash Titans insured?

Yes — Trash Titans is fully insured for both residential and commercial junk removal jobs across Middle Tennessee. You can hire with confidence knowing your property is protected.

Do you offer curbside pickup in Murfreesboro?

Absolutely! Just place your items at the curb and we'll load and haul everything away. You don't even need to be home. It's one of our most popular and convenient services across Middle Tennessee.

What areas do you serve besides Murfreesboro?

Trash Titans serves Murfreesboro, Nashville, Smyrna, LaVergne, Nolensville, Franklin, Lebanon and Mt. Juliet Tennessee. Not sure if we cover your area? Call or text 615-987-9876 and we'll let you know — we may still be able to help.

Do you haul away old appliances in Murfreesboro?

Yes — refrigerators, washers, dryers, dishwashers, stoves — we haul them all. No need to drag them to the curb yourself. We load and remove appliances from anywhere in your home across Murfreesboro and all of Middle Tennessee.

Can you do same day junk removal?

We love same day jobs and will always try to make it happen. Our availability depends on our current schedule so the sooner you reach out the better. Text us at 615-987-9876 and we'll let you know what we can do — we move fast when we can.

Do I need to be home for junk pickup?

For outdoor jobs you don't need to be home at all — just show us what needs to go and we'll handle the rest. For indoor jobs we're happy to work unsupervised but that's ultimately your call as the customer. Whatever makes you most comfortable works for us.

Do you donate or recycle items?

We try to recycle and repurpose whenever possible. If an item can find a new home we'll do our best to make that happen. We're conscientious about keeping usable items out of the landfill — it's the right thing to do.
