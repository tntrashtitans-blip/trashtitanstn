Our Story

Built By Hustle.
Driven By Results.

Lawson Gregory is a junior at Oakland High School in Murfreesboro, Tennessee — and he's already running a business that most adults would be proud of. Trash Titans didn't start with investors or a business plan. It started with a kid who saw a need in his community, grabbed a flatbed trailer, and got to work.

Every dollar Lawson earned went straight back into the business. That flatbed became a full-size dump trailer. Those first few neighbors became hundreds of satisfied customers across eight cities — from Murfreesboro driveways to Nashville rooftops and everywhere in between.

"Jobs like this remind me how far hustle and hard work can take you." — Lawson Gregory

Trash Titans is fully insured and serves both residential and commercial customers across Middle Tennessee. When you hire Lawson, you're not calling a faceless corporation. You're supporting a local young entrepreneur who will show up on time, work hard, and make sure you're 100% satisfied — every single job.

We haul almost anything — old furniture, appliances, garage junk, renovation debris, yard waste, storm damage, office equipment, and more. No job is too big, too small, or too unusual.

How quickly can you pick up my junk?

We offer same-week service in most cases. Fill out our quote form or text us and we'll get you scheduled fast.

Yes — Trash Titans is fully insured for both residential and commercial junk removal jobs across Middle Tennessee.

Absolutely. Just place your items at the curb and we'll load and haul everything away. You don't even need to be home.

Can you do same day junk removal?
