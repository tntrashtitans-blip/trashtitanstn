Franklin's Local Junk Removal Crew

Franklin and Williamson County are home to some of Middle Tennessee's most established and fastest-growing neighborhoods. From the historic homes near Downtown Franklin and Leiper's Fork to the newer builds in Westhaven, Fieldstone Farms, and Berry Farms, we've hauled junk in all of them.

Franklin homeowners tend to have higher expectations — and that's fine with us. We show up on time, work cleanly, and leave the space broom-ready. We're also mindful of HOA rules in Williamson County — careful about parking, turnaround time, and keeping the job tidy.

Franklin is about 25 minutes from our Murfreesboro base, which means we can usually fit Franklin jobs in on the same routing day as Brentwood, Spring Hill, or Nolensville stops. Text 615-987-9876 — most Franklin jobs get quoted and scheduled within 24 hours.
